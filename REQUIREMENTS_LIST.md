## Risk Assessment

### High Priority Risks
- **Regulatory compliance** in target markets
- **Security audit delays** affecting launch timeline
- **Legal entity setup** complexity in multiple jurisdictions
- **App store approval** timelines and rejection risks

### Mitigation Strategies
- Early engagement with legal and compliance experts
- Multiple security audit provider options
- Jurisdictional analysis for optimal entity structure
- Compliance-first app store preparation
